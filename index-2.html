How the scoring works
ROI Score = ((Annual Cost Saved ÷ 1,000) + Error Impact) × Complexity Discount
Annual Cost = Hrs/week × 52 × Hourly rate
Error Impact = Error rate × Frequency weight (Daily=5, Weekly=1, Monthly=0.25) × 10
Complexity = Low (1.0×) · Medium (0.7×) · High (0.4×) — discounts harder-to-automate processes
